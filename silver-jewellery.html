Silver Jewellery Care Tips

Avoid Water

Remove silver jewellery while bathing, swimming, or washing hands to prevent tarnish and water spots.

No Chemicals

Avoid contact with perfumes, hairspray, lotions, and household chemicals that can damage silver.

Regular Cleaning

Polish with a soft silver cloth to maintain shine and prevent tarnish. Store in airtight bags.
